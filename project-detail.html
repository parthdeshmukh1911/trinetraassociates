Project Overview

Tech Tower is a landmark commercial development in the heart of Mumbai's prime business district. This 25-storey Grade A office building redefines modern workspace with cutting-edge infrastructure, sustainable design, and world-class amenities.

Strategically located in Bandra Kurla Complex with excellent connectivity to major business hubs, Tech Tower offers premium office spaces ranging from 5,000 to 50,000 sq.ft, designed to meet the needs of modern enterprises.
